Christine Røde

is a designer doing product design and branding. She's fascinated the intersection of traditional craft with new technology, and likes work that includes both programming and scissors alike.

She's lived and worked in Oslo, San Francisco and London, and currently resides in Amsterdam. Until recently, she designed products for Facebook. Today, she just wants to make things that delight*.
